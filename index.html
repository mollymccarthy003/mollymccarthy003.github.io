Hello, I'm Molly!

I'm an aspiring art educator, student and artist exploring the internet and making stuff for people to enjoy! I am exploring many mediums but my favorites so far have been linocut printmaking and ceramics. Thanks for checking me out!
Girls, sitting in my bed, watching Rupaul's Drag Race, Littlest Pet Shop, Fortnite, My Little Pony, Animal Crossing, Attack on Titan, and my cat Martha are usually on my mind day to day. When I am not studying or at work, these are my lifelines and inspire so much of what I create!
I've been selling my linocut patches, totes and bandanas on Etsy for about a year now. My patches have a lot of customization options and inviduality to each of them. Since they are hand stamped they are all a little different! Much of 2025 was about getting the ball rolling, I can't wait to continue to share my creations this year!
Thanks for stopping by! Have a good day!💜
