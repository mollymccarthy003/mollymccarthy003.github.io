About Linocut Printing

Linocut printing is a relief printing technique that involves carving an image into a sheet of linoleum. The raised areas are inked and pressed onto paper, creating bold, graphic prints with distinctive character.

This art form gained popularity in the early 20th century and has been embraced by artists worldwide for its accessibility and expressive potential. From simple designs to complex multi-color prints, linocut offers endless creative possibilities.

The process combines traditional craftsmanship with artistic vision, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced printmakers. Each print carries the unique marks of hand-carved blocks, ensuring that no two impressions are exactly alike.
