Hello, I'm Molly!

I'm an aspiring art educator, student and artist exploring the internet and making stuff for people to enjoy! I am exploring many mediums but my favorites have been linocut printmaking and ceramics. Thanks for checking me out!

Girls, sitting in my bed, watching Rupaul's Drag Race, Littlest Pet Shop, Fortnite, My Little Pony, Animal Crossing, Attack on Titan, and my cat Martha are just a few things that consume my mind. When I am not studying or at work, these are my lifelines and inspire so much of what I create!

I've been selling my linocut patches, totes and bandanas on Etsy for about a year now. Linocut has a lot of customization options and inviduality to each creation. Since they are hand stamped, they are all a little different! 2025 was about getting started and I can't wait to continue to share my creations this year!

Thanks for stopping by!💜
